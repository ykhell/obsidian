	- $\Bbb EX = \lambda$
	- $\mathbf{Var}(X) = \lambda$

	-  $\Bbb EX =np$
- $X \sim \mathrm{Exp}(\lambda),$
	 - $\Bbb EX = \frac{1}{\lambda}$
	 - $\mathbf{Var}(X) = \frac 1{\lambda^2}$

	- $\Bbb EX = \mu$
	- $\mathbf{Var}(X) = \sigma^2$
